Disconnect Stops syncing on this device. Your data on Drive and this device are kept.

Orbit stores one file in your own Google Drive (orbit-data.json) and syncs it across the devices you sign in on. Edits are last-write-wins — if you edit two devices offline at the same time, the last one to sync overwrites the other.

Google Calendar Push your events to a dedicated "Orbit" calendar.

Orbit creates an "Orbit" calendar in your Google account and keeps it in sync with your events here. Edit or delete an event in Orbit and the change follows; editing directly in Google Calendar does not sync back.
